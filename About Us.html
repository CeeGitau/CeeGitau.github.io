ABOUT US
Our Founders established a dream that is ingrained in our DNA from modest beginnings in 1989 and a strong regard for nature and community. The company has a
lovely history of how families, friends, and the strength of the human spirit came together to build Kenya's premier collection of privately owned, great hotels and resorts.
As a long-standing member of Kenya's hospitality sector, we have amassed a portfolio of great hotels throughout the years. Three distinctive buildings are housed by Getaway
Deluxe in prominent locations; each is harmoniously unique to its own history and environment, strengthened by a desire to influence neighboring communities and create a
sustainable future.


At Getaway Deluxe, we take Customer Service very seriously. Customer Satisfaction is our number one priority. Our experiences are designed to show you the grandeur of nature, the wonders
of Africa, delicious food, and our unquestionably welcoming hospitality.
